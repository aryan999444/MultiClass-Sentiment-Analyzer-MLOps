Project Story: The Imbalance Challenge

The Problem: Skewed Data

Our model was trained on the Flipkart Reviews dataset, which presented an extreme case of **class imbalance**. With the vast majority of reviews being positive, the model naturally learned to prioritize the positive class, resulting in poor performance (near zero F1-score) on the underrepresented **Neutral** class.

Dataset Distribution

Positive Reviews
81%
Neutral Reviews (Critical Minority)
5%
Negative Reviews
14%

The challenge: Improving neutral sentiment detection despite severe class imbalance

The Breakthrough

By applying a meticulously calculated class weighting strategy during model training, we effectively penalized misclassifications of the neutral sentiment. This fundamental MLOps refinement resulted in the significant F1-score increase, ensuring the model is valuable across all sentiment categories in production.
